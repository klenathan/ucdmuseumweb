Wuying Pagoda

  • Material: Stone
  • Date: 13th century
  • Found in: Wuchang, Wuhan, Hubei Province
  • Description: The pagoda has eight sides and seven storeys, with four of the storeys including carvings of Buddhist figures carved in small niches.[7] It is 11.25 meters tall, with a diameter of 4.25 meters at the "Sumeru Throne" style base.
